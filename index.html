An unexamined life is not worth living, and the three axes that triangulate and direct Victoria's are impact, knowledge, and empathy.

Victoria is studing Economics and Computer Science at Stanford, and she is passionate about education, financial access, emerging markets, and public interest technology.

Upon coming to Stanford from Chengdu, China, she has explored both public and private sectors. From serving as a research analyst in European Policy Institute in Skopje, North Macedonia on economic policy, to as a BD China Associate for a non-profit promoting education and design thinking, she has become increasingly interested in technology and the private sector. Victoria has been working on and leading projects in Stanford Consulting for the past two years, which took her to her last summer internship at Gusto, a unicorn SF-based startup, as corporate development intern working on product and partnership strategy. Most recently, she enjoys learning to build this website, learning algorithms and applying it to market design problems, and reading philosophy.

Victoria is actively looking for opportunitis in strategy, venture capital, data analytics, and startups during the school year 2020-2021. She is always ready to get her hands dirty and solve challenging problems that lays her way.
